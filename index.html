Palestine is a country rich in history and heritage, and it has a variety of tourist attractions

Al-Aqsa Mosque

AlQuds: The holy city forMuslims, it houses many important religious sites, such as the Al-Aqsa Mosque .

Haifa

Haifa: A beautiful coastal city, it houses many historical and cultural sites, such as the Haifa Museum of Art .

Nablus

Nablus: The second-largest city in Palestine, it houses many historical and archaeological sites, such as the Saladin Citadel

Hebron

Hebron: An ancient city located in the West Bank, it houses many historical and archaeological sites, such as the Ibrahimi Mosque and the Church of the Nativity.

The Palestinian Mountains:

They are characterized by their picturesque nature and environmental diversity, and they house many tourist destinations, such as the Carmel Mountain Reserve and Wadi Rum.
